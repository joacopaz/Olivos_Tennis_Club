Welcome to our club

Olivos Tennis Club was first founded in 1920 by John and Erika Wilkins with the objective of promoting excellence and sportsmanship in high-end competitive tennis.

With 20 original members, the club grew steadily up to 2000 members in 2022, with 5 new subsidiaries and cutting-edge training grounds we strive to bring the best out of our members.

You can become one too!
